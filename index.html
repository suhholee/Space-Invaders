HOW TO PLAY
  • Choose a STAR Premier League player to start the game.
  • Win THREE AWAY GAMES for the Premier League title!
  • Move your player left ← or right →, and shoot using the space bar.
  • An opposing Premier League team will try to shoot the ball to hit you.
  • When you get hit by their ball, 50 points are deducted and you will lose a life. You have 3 lives.
  • Your mission is to defeat all the opponents by hitting them with the football.
  • Your 3 lives WILL NOT be renewed each level.
  • Press the arrow button on the bottom right to progress to the next round.
  • Win the Premier League title by defeating all three opponents!
